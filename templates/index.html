DrowsyRide

Drive safe,

because we care.

Feeling tired and sleepy is ok, but while driving? That's a big risk. Being on road its all about focus, but sometimes that too is lost when one's worn out. With DrowsyRide™ advanced technology, it warms the driver with an alarm sound when he's feeling drowsy or taking too much yawn. One alert can save the rest of your life.
